Introduction

Meal Planning can be difficult. Especially if you have goals, formulating a plan that fits your needs, preferences, and schedule can be quite a challenge. Let’s see if GPT can create a realistic meal plan for me, a adjust it basede on my goals and tastes.

Let’s Go!

Prompt: Generate a meal plan for my day

Response: Here’s a balanced meal plan for your day:

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

  • Scrambled eggs with spinach and feta
  • Whole-grain toast
  • A small bowl of mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • A cup of black coffee or green tea

Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)

  • Greek yogurt with honey and almonds
  • A small banana

Lunch (12:30 PM)

  • Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and roasted vegetables (zucchini, bell peppers, and carrots)
  • A side of mixed greens with olive oil and balsamic vinegar
  • Sparkling water or herbal tea

Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM)

  • Hummus with sliced cucumber and whole-wheat crackers
  • A handful of walnuts

Dinner (6:30 PM)

  • Baked salmon with lemon and herbs
  • Steamed asparagus and mashed sweet potatoes
  • A glass of water with lemon

Evening Snack (8:30 PM, optional)

  • A small portion of dark chocolate and a handful of almonds
  • Chamomile tea

Would you like any modifications based on your dietary preferences or goals?

Notes * Right off the bat, GPT generated a detailed and realistic meal plan for a day, including time stamps for each step. * It also built in both meals and snacks to prevent me from going hungry * The foods generated are all very healthy, featuring many fruits and vegetabls throughout the day, balanced with a healthy amount of protein and carbs. * I assume the “for my day” part of the prompt is what led GPT to give time recommendations for each meal/snack * Overall, GPT immediately generated an extremely solid meal plan for a day

Prompt: I want to lose weight

Response: For weight loss, I’ll adjust your meal plan to be nutrient-dense, high in protein, and lower in calories while keeping you full and satisfied.

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

  • 2 boiled or scrambled egg whites + 1 whole egg
  • Sautéed spinach and mushrooms
  • 1 slice of whole-grain toast
  • Black coffee or green tea (no sugar)

Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)

  • Low-fat Greek yogurt with a few almonds and chia seeds
  • Half an apple

Lunch (12:30 PM)

  • Grilled chicken breast or tofu
  • Cauliflower rice or a small portion of quinoa
  • Roasted Brussels sprouts and bell peppers
  • Mixed greens with lemon vinaigrette

Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM)

  • Cucumber, celery, and carrot sticks with hummus
  • Green tea

Dinner (6:30 PM)

  • Baked salmon or lean turkey
  • Steamed broccoli and roasted sweet potatoes (small portion)
  • Herbal tea or water with lemon

Evening Snack (8:30 PM, optional)

  • A handful of almonds or a small protein smoothie (unsweetened almond milk, protein powder, spinach)

This plan keeps you in a calorie deficit while providing enough protein, fiber, and healthy fats to keep you full. Let me know if you have any preferences or restrictions!

Notes

  • Based on the new goal I provided of losing weight, GPT modified its meal plan accordingly
  • It made substtiitutions such as egg-whites and low-fat Greek yogurt to go towards my goal. The original plan already featured mostly healthy foods, so I did not expect to see a huge amount of change
  • It added a note at the end about how this updated plan helps me reach my goal, which was very helpful

Prompt: I’m Asian

Response: Here’s a weight-loss-friendly meal plan incorporating Asian flavors and ingredients:

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

  • Miso soup with tofu and seaweed
  • 1 boiled egg
  • A small bowl of steamed brown rice (or cauliflower rice for lower carbs)
  • Green tea (no sugar)

Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)

  • Edamame with a sprinkle of sea salt
  • A small serving of unsweetened soy milk

Lunch (12:30 PM)

  • Grilled or steamed fish (salmon or mackerel)
  • Stir-fried bok choy and shiitake mushrooms with garlic
  • A small bowl of multigrain rice
  • Kimchi or pickled vegetables

Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM)

  • Unsalted roasted nuts (almonds or walnuts)
  • A cup of matcha (without sugar)

Dinner (6:30 PM)

  • Chicken or tofu stir-fry with broccoli, bell peppers, and ginger
  • Steamed cauliflower rice or konjac noodles
  • Side of miso soup

Evening Snack (8:30 PM, optional)

  • A small serving of unsweetened yogurt with chia seeds
  • Herbal tea

This meal plan aligns with traditional Asian cuisine while supporting your weight-loss goals. Let me know if you want to adjust anything!

Notes * A bit of a curveball here, but I wanted to see how GPT would modify the plan for a particular taste. However, instead of asking it to explicitly change the plan, I simply stated that I am Asian to see if GPT would modify its plan to fit an Asian diet. * GPT did, in fact, modify the plan to fit an Asian diet, now featuring items like Miso soup, bok choy, and matcha. * In its conclusion statement, it made sure to mention that this plan appeals to both my Asian taste and my weight-loss goals, showing that it remembered details from my previous prompts.

Prompt: I like ice cream and burgers and chicken nuggets and French fries

You can still enjoy your favorite foods while losing weight by making healthier versions of them. Here’s how we can incorporate them into a balanced meal plan:

Breakfast (8:00 AM)

  • Scrambled eggs with spinach and a drizzle of soy sauce
  • A small whole-grain wrap or multigrain toast
  • Green tea (no sugar)

Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)

  • Greek yogurt with a few berries and chia seeds
  • A handful of almonds

Lunch (12:30 PM)

  • Healthy Burger:
    • Grilled lean chicken or turkey patty (or tofu patty)
    • Whole wheat bun (or lettuce wrap for fewer carbs)
    • Loaded with veggies (tomato, lettuce, onions)
    • A light spread of Greek yogurt or avocado instead of mayo
  • A side of baked sweet potato fries

Afternoon Snack (3:30 PM)

  • Roasted chickpeas or edamame
  • A small matcha latte (unsweetened)

Dinner (6:30 PM)

  • Healthier Chicken Nuggets & Fries:
    • Air-fried or baked chicken nuggets (made with lean chicken and almond flour or whole wheat breadcrumbs)
    • Air-fried or baked French fries (sweet potatoes or regular potatoes)
    • A side of steamed greens (bok choy, broccoli, or spinach)

Evening Treat (8:30 PM, optional)

  • Healthier Ice Cream Alternative:
    • Blended frozen banana with a little cocoa powder and peanut butter
    • Or a small portion of low-calorie ice cream

This way, you get to enjoy your favorite foods in a healthier way while still supporting your weight loss goals! Let me know if you’d like any adjustments.

Notes * This time, I gave GPT what I thought was a very difficult task: liking fast food while still wanting to achieve weight loss * GPT, however, handled the prompt very well, recommending healthier alternatives to each of the fast food items I mentioned. * It also included slightly more detail regarding preparation in this meal plan, likely to differentiate these healthier alternatives from their fast food counterparts. * It gave up on incorporating Asian cuisine, likely because I switched to non-Asian specifications for my food * In the conclusion, it once again explained how the plan allows me to enjoy the foods I like while still supporting my weight loss goals, emphasizing its memory of my goals

Conclusion

Overall, I was very impressed with GPT’s generated meal plans, which were both detailed and realistic upon the first prompt. As I asked it to modify the plan to fit certain goals (weight loss) and food preferences (Asian cuisine, typically unhealthy foods), it modified the plan accordingly, making sureee to stay in line with my goals upon each modification.
